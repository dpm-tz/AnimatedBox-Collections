Who We Are?

dpmacademy is a non-profit organization dedicated to the conservation of wildlife in Mbeya, Tanzania. Our mission is to protect and preserve the natural habitats of endangered species, promote sustainable living, and create a harmonious relationship between humans and wildlife.
We work closely with local communities, government agencies, and other conservation organizations to achieve our goals. Our team of experts includes wildlife biologists, ecologists, and conservationists who are passionate about protecting the environment and wildlife. Join us in our mission to make a positive impact on the world.
